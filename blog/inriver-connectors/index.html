Aware announces the release of 4 inRiver connectors

﻿
Trevor Olson
23 May 2017
inRiverAwarePIMProduct Data

Aware taps the power of the best-in-breed technology platforms to enable marketing strategies and build technology for engaging digital experiences. We’re pleased to announce the release of four new Aware built inRiver connectors.

Aware Sitecore + inRIver Connector
Aware Salesforce + inRiver Connector
Aware Azure / Amazon Web Services +inRiver Connector
Aware CloudCraze+inRiver Connector

Aware Sitecore + inRIver Connector PRO

Publish product data from inRiver into Sitecore with this simple and configurable connector.

Connector Highlights

  • Maintains product relationships, hierarchies, items and categories from inRiver to Sitecore
  • Publishes products into multiple Sitecore websites and store views
  • Leverages Sitecore best practices for content structure
  • Designed to easily import custom product attributes into Sitecore
  • Built to move large amounts of product data from inRiver to Sitecore
  • View products prior to being published to production within Sitecore Staging environment
  • Schedule product data publishing times into Sitecore
  • Highly configurable for specific customer needs

inRiver version supported: inRiver 6.x

Sitecore version supported: Sitecore 7.0+

Aware Salesforce+inRiver Connector
Publish product data from inRiver into Salesforce with this simple and configurable connector.
Connector Highlights

  • Maintains product relationships, hierarchies, items and categories from inRiver to Salesforce
  • Connector is customizable to push content into any type of Salesforce field mapping or for other specific needs
  • Enables CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) Salesforce features for your sales reps or channel members to leverage up-to-the-second inRiver product content from within Salesforce
  • Enables use of Cloud Craze B2B Ecommerce running on Salesforce to monetize your inRiver product content
  • Every inRiver save action can trigger a publish to Salesforce or it can be configured to only publish items that meet inRiver completeness rules
  • Built to move large amounts of product data from inRiver to Salesforce
  • Includes a logging function to troubleshoot product information transfer
  • Publishes products to multiple Salesforce orgs
  • Supports publishing to Salesforce Dev/Staging environments to view products before they are published to production

inRiver version supported: inRiver 6.x
