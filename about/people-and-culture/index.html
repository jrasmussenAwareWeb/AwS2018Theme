We have a team of smart and talented people in energy-infused culture that enables our employees to do their best work.

Aware provides more than just your average career. Each Awarian is encouraged to expand their energy on what they’re passionate about – not simply scrape the surface of what they’re good at.

Top Ten Reasons to Work at Aware

10. We are a virtual company which means the only commuting needed is from your bed to your desk. We also have a very snazzy office in Minneapolis so the coffee shop isn’t the only place that allows you to switch-up your scenery.

9. Employees are given some pretty sweet tech tools to ensure their productivity and happiness from day one - including a laptop, extra monitors, an iPhone and access to additional resources as needed.

Awarians tend to be smart, funny and accountable - it’s the desirable coworker trifecta.

8. Every day is “Bring Your Pet to Work Day,” and some furry friends have even been deemed office assistants.

7. Our clients are pretty great and they give us the opportunity to work on some impressive projects, which means we’re always putting our talents to good use.

6. Maintaining transparent and open communication within the workplace is important to us – around here we don’t close doors.

5. Work-life balance is an art form we have mastered – just check out our photos if you need verification.

4. We only hire the best, but we have a way of making the best the greatest. With ongoing training and the opportunity to work alongside some of the industry’s brightest, our employees soar to new heights.

The best thing about working at Aware is the variety that comes with each client.

3. No man is an island - we may be a virtual organization (thus saving you moolah in the gas department) but we do a grand job of communicating and collaborating as one giant Awarian team.

2. We’ve received 5 Sitecore Site of the Year awards, 17 MVP nods, and we're one of the largest Sitecore partners in North America. Who doesn’t want to be on the winning team?

1. Our equation to running a flourishing company is simple: Happy Employees = Beyond Satisfied Clients =  A Really Successful Company.
